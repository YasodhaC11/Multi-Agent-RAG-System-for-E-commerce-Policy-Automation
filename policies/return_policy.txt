Section 1: Eligibility
Customers may return items within 7 days of delivery.
Items must be unused, undamaged, and in original packaging.

Section 2: Non-Returnable Items
The following items are not eligible for return:
- Perishable goods (food, flowers)
- Personal hygiene products
- Customized or personalized items

Section 3: Exceptions
If a non-returnable item is delivered in damaged condition, a refund or replacement may be issued.

Section 4: Return Process
Customers must initiate a return request via the platform within the return window.
