Section 1: Shipping Time
Orders are shipped within 24–48 hours.

Section 2: Delivery Timeline
Metro: 2–4 days
Non-metro: 4–7 days

Section 3: Delays
Delays may occur due to logistics, weather, or external factors.

Possible Reasons (why the order shows as 'delivered')
If your order consisted of multiple items and only a few were delivered to you, it's possible that the other items in your order were dispatched in another shipment.

Your family members/ neighbors or reception might have collected the package on your behalf.

My order says 'delivered', but I didn't receive it. What should I do?
If your package is marked as delivered but you haven't received the package, we recommend using the self-serve option in your account to submit a query.

Note:

To help us take timely action, please report the issue within 7 days from the date of delivery.
Queries submitted after 7 days are not eligible for rescue.
Pay on Delivery orders are not eligible for rescue, please place a new order.

How can I report a package that says delivered, but not received?
To report a package that shows as delivered but hasn't been received, please follow these steps:

Go to Your Account and select "Contact Us".
Choose the “Chat with Us ” option.
Describe the issue as “Package not received.”
Select the item that is marked as delivered.
Follow the on-screen instructions and you’ll be given the option to “Raise a Query.
Review the summary of your issue and select Submit.
We will investigate the issue and keep you updated through your account.
